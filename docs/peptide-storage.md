## Research Use Only Notice
All materials supplied by NexXGEN Peptides are intended **strictly for laboratory research use only (RUO)**.  
These materials are **not intended for human consumption, veterinary use, cosmetic application, or medical treatment**.

Proper storage of peptide materials is essential for maintaining chemical integrity and preventing degradation.

---

# General Storage Principles
Peptides are sensitive to:
- temperature fluctuations
- moisture exposure
- oxidation
- repeated freeze-thaw cycles

Improper storage may result in structural degradation or loss of experimental consistency.

---

# Recommended Storage Conditions

## Lyophilized (Powder) Peptides
**Short term:** 2°C to 8°C (refrigerated)  
**Long term:** -20°C or lower  

Protect from heat, humidity, and direct light.

## Reconstituted Peptides
Once reconstituted, stability typically decreases.  
Typical research practice is storage at **-20°C to -80°C**.

Researchers often divide solutions into **aliquots** to reduce freeze–thaw exposure.

---

# Avoiding Degradation
Researchers should minimize:
- repeated thawing and freezing
- prolonged exposure to room temperature
- exposure to oxygen and light
- contamination during handling

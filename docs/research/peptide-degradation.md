---

# What Causes Peptide Degradation

Peptides may degrade due to several factors including:

- temperature fluctuations
- oxidation
- hydrolysis
- light exposure
- repeated freeze-thaw cycles

Maintaining controlled laboratory conditions is essential for preserving peptide integrity.

---

# Preventing Degradation

Researchers often minimize degradation by:

- storing peptides at low temperatures
- protecting samples from light
- avoiding repeated freeze-thaw cycles
- using sterile laboratory handling procedures
